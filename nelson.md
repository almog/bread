# Nelson Bread
##Formula
* 35% Honey
* 100% Seeds (Sesame, Flax, Wheat Bran, Sunflower Seeds, Pumpkin Seeds)
* 95% Water
* 2.5% Salt
* 50% Whole wheat flour
* 50% White flour
* 30% Firm starter

##Quantities for one loaf
* 70gr Honey
* 200gr Seeds (Sesame, Flax, Wheat Bran, Sunflower Seeds, Pumpkin Seeds)
* 170gr Water
* 5gr Salt
* 100gr Whole wheat flour
* 100gr White flour
* 80gr Firm starter
